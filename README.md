## 👨‍💻 About Me

I'm a Software Engineer at **MindRuby Technologies** in Indore, India — building full-stack web platforms and **agentic AI products** end-to-end. Over 2+ years I've shipped childcare SaaS with Stripe, an enterprise AI assistant powered by LangChain RAG and LangGraph, an AI voice-agent SaaS for real-estate training over VAPI/WebRTC, and a tyre-industry operations platform — alongside 13+ personal projects live in production.

- 🤖 Currently building **multi-agent systems** with LangGraph, tool calling, and chain-of-thought
- 🎙️ Working on **real-time voice AI** pipelines (VAPI · WebRTC · STT/TTS · VAD)
- 🧠 Exploring RAG over documents, SQL/NoSQL, CRM, and the open web
- ✨ Sweating the details on motion-rich frontends — GSAP, Three.js, Framer Motion
- 📍 Indore, India · Open to collaboration on AI products and ambitious frontend work
